UNLV IEEE Student Branch

Events Calendar

We have multiple activities throughout the year such as technical workshops, resume workshops, technical seminars, guest speakers, and conferences. The IEEE has many competitions (regional, national, international) that student members can compete in, but the main competitions our branch has focused on are the IEEEXtreme 24-hour programming competition held every fall, and the Student Paper Competition and Micromouse competition held every spring.

Past Workshops

Matlab Workshop

Presenter: Justin Le

Time: October 28, 2015 @ 5:30PM
Where: TBE B-348

More Information

This hands-on workshop covers basic skills necessary for students to use MATLAB productively in their coursework and research projects.
The demonstrations will highlight the underlying mechanisms of the program and the language's useful but often neglected features, with an emphasis on common mathematics used in engineering.

The main points of discussion include:

  • fundamentals
  • debugging tools
  • best practices
  • general tips for users at every level of experience
  • and much more!
