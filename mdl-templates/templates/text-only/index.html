Introduction

I am a senior at University of Nevada, Las Vegas studying Electrical Engineering with a minor in Applied Mathematics. Technology excites me and I constantly challenge myself to learn more and more. I am not content with simply learning how to use technology, I need to understand how it operates down to the atomic scale, a trait which led me to pursue engineering. My ambition drives me to research topics more in depth than my classes would usually cover, especially when it comes to communications, solid state devices, embedded systems, hardware design, electromagnetism, and modern physics.

I am a hard working fast learner who excels when I get thrown into a project that most would consider to be "over my head". I always think many steps ahead during my design process and consider the bigger picture when it come to the implementation of my design..
